About Us

R&G's chocolate started as an idea for a style guide assignment by Ryan and Gabe in IAT 399. We have since kept the website and have transitioned to full time chocolatiers. As our brand and company grows, so do our love for chocolate. We provide the finest chocolate for afforadable prices. Our chocolate is perfect for gifts, parties, or even a night of guilty pleasure. Browse our many creations here and fall as deeply in love with our chocolates as we do.
