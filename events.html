Upcoming Events

R&G's Chocolate has a multitude of fun and exciting events that anyone can come and enjoy. Check out our event listings here to learn more about the events.

Chocolate Tasting

  • Date: November 2nd, 2019
  • Location: Canada Place
  • Time: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Come try our new experimental chocolates! Our gourmet chefs have been innovating and redefining the very nature of fine chocolate. With never-before-seen kinds of chocolatey sweets, you won't be able to stop yourself from grabbing a couple extra pieces. This free event will attract many chocolate enthusiasts in Vancouver, so be sure to come on by nice and early before we run out!

Chocolate Making Class

  • Date: December 11th, 2019
  • Location: R&G's Chocolate | Main Offices
  • Time: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Ticket Price: $24.99

Our master chefs invite you to join them to learn how we make our chocolate. After honing their craft for years, we feel that it is important for our chefs to share their knowledge. Making chocolate is an extremely fun and social event that is best done with a partner or a group. So bring a partner or two and dive into the science of chocolate.

Santa's Cookies Factory

  • Date: December 22nd, 2019
  • Location: Vancouver Pacific Centre
  • Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

We have a contract with old Saint Nick himself. We are Santa's personal cookie provider. When he can't get the delicious cookies that you make for him on Christmas Eve, we ship out large orders of our famous chocolate chip cookies for him to enjoy, which tides him over until the following christmas. We want to share these cookies with you, bring your family and come see how we make our chocolate chip cookies (and have a few samples) for free, of course!
